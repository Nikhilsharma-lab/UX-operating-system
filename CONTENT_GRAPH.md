## Purpose

This is the knowledge map of the Nikhil Sharma Executive Knowledge System.

It is not a sitemap.

It documents how ideas connect, so the website behaves like a knowledge network rather than a collection of isolated pages.

Every new page should declare:

1. Inbound links
2. Outbound links
3. Related transformation stories
4. Related frameworks
5. Related essays
6. Related operating manual pages
7. Potential future formats
