## Purpose

Defines the depth layers of the experience by time investment, so the site rewards a 10-second skim and a 2-hour deep read equally well.

## The Layers

```txt
Layer 1 — 10 seconds — Identity
Layer 2 — 5 minutes — Proof
Layer 3 — 30 minutes — Thinking
Layer 4 — 2 hours — Operating System
```

## How To Apply

- Layer 1 must land identity instantly through hierarchy and copy.
- Layer 2 must surface proof without requiring deep reading.
- Layer 3 rewards the curious with thinking and frameworks.
- Layer 4 rewards the committed with a full operating system.
