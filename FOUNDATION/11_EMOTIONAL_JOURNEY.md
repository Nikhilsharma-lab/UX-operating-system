# 11_EMOTIONAL_JOURNEY.md

## Purpose

Defines the emotional arc a senior visitor moves through while experiencing the Nikhil Sharma Executive Knowledge System. Every page should advance the visitor along this journey.

## Priority

## The Journey

```txt
Minute 0: This feels different.
Minute 2: This person has done meaningful work.
Minute 5: This person thinks differently.
Minute 10: I want this person on my leadership team.
Minute 30: I am stealing these ideas.
```

## How To Apply

- Minute 0 is carried by the homepage hero, typography, and restraint.
- Minute 2 is carried by transformation stories and evidence.
- Minute 5 is carried by frameworks and the operating manual.
- Minute 10 is carried by decision quality and judgment.
- Minute 30 is carried by teachable, reusable systems.
