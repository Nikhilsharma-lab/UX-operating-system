# Expanded Positioning

Nikhil Sharma is a senior product design executive operating at the intersection of design leadership, product strategy, AI-native workflows, organizational design, fintech, and entrepreneurship.

He builds product organizations that turn ambiguity into measurable business outcomes.

His work is not limited to designing interfaces.

It includes building teams, operating models, design systems, AI-assisted product development workflows, research practices, trust systems, growth systems, and founder-led products.

# Positioning Thesis

Most design portfolios show finished work.

This system should show how high-performing product organizations are built.

The positioning is not:

> I design great products.

The positioning is:

> I build the systems that make great products possible.
