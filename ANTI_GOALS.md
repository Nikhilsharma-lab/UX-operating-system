# ANTI_GOALS.md

## Purpose

This file defines what the Nikhil Sharma Executive Knowledge System must never become.

Anti-goals protect the project from drift.

---

## We Will Not Build

### 1. A Behance Portfolio

No screenshot galleries without thinking.

### 2. A Dribbble Profile

No visual polish without business context.

### 3. A Generic UX Case Study Site

No repetitive structure of problem, process, solution, impact unless it is elevated into a transformation narrative.

### 4. A Resume Website

Chronology is not identity.

### 5. A Trendy AI Showcase

AI is not the point. Organizational transformation is the point.

### 6. A Blog of Random Opinions

Every piece of writing should teach, clarify, or provide a reusable framework.

### 7. A Personal Brand Vanity Site

The goal is usefulness, not self-celebration.

### 8. A Visual Design Showcase Without Thinking

Craft matters, but judgment matters more.

---

## Forbidden Patterns

Avoid:

- “I’m passionate about…”
- “World-class design leader…”
- “Visionary…”
- “Cutting-edge…”
- “Human-centered designer…”
- “Pixel-perfect…”
- Unexplained screenshots
- Generic portfolio cards
- Floating buzzwords
- Fancy motion without meaning
- Case studies that hide decisions
- Metrics that cannot survive interview scrutiny

---

## Governing Rule

If a page does not teach, prove, clarify, or demonstrate judgment, it should not exist.
